中国驻葡萄牙大使馆：赵本堂大使出席中葡气候与能源联合研究院签约暨揭牌仪式

Ambassador Zhao Bentang attended the signing and opening ceremony of Sino-Portuguese Joint Research Institute of Climate and Energy

2024年10月11日，中国驻葡萄牙大使赵本堂出席中国石油大学（北京）与里斯本高等理工学院共建中葡气候与能源联合研究院签约暨揭牌仪式并致辞。

英文原文见来源链接。以下为使馆官网英文报道原文：

On October 11, 2024, the Ambassador of the People's Republic of China, Dr.Zhao Bentang attended the signing and launching ceremony of the Sino-Portuguese Joint Institute of Climate and Energy between China University of Petroleum (Beijing) and Lisbon Institute of Technology and delivered a speech. Prof. Rogerio Colaco, president of the Institute of Technology of the University of Lisbon, Prof. Bao Zhidong, Vice-rector of China University of Petroleum (Beijing), and relevant Chinese and Portuguese experts and scholars attended the event.

Ambassador Dr. Zhao Bentang pointed out that in the context of global carbon neutrality, the importance of using high-tech means such as artificial intelligence to promote the development of clean energy and respond to climate change has become increasingly prominent, and the friendly cooperation between China and Portugal has contributed to Portugal's outstanding achievements in the field of clean energy. Ambassador Dr.Zhao Bentang encouraged young Chinese and Portuguese scientists to deepen friendly cooperation and write a new chapter in the 500-year traditional friendship between China and Portugal.
