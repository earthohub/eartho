廖宗湖教授团队获批国家重点研发计划“政府间国际科技创新合作”重点专项

National key R&D project on international S&T cooperation approved

廖宗湖教授团队获批国家重点研发计划“政府间国际科技创新合作”重点专项项目，与中葡气候与能源领域的国际合作研究密切相关。详情请参见校内新闻发布。

The team led by Prof. Liao Zonghu was approved for a National Key R&D Programme project on government international science and technology cooperation, supporting China–Portugal climate and energy research.
